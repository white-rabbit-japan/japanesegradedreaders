Kanji Flashcard Highlights

  • Enjoy reading Japanese texts without reaching for a dictionary.

How it works

Through the use of word repetition, familiar contexts, dynamic audio, and vivid illustrations, our graded reader books are designed to allow you to read a book from start to finish, without referring to a dictionary, while learning new words and grammar effortlessly along the way.

Stimulate your senses

Each story comes with professionally illustrated and audio- recorded content that brings the stories to life. No bland, outdated drawings or lifeless audio.

Connect the dots

Learn like a child. Books for Japanese children are written with their reading level and interests in mind. Why should it be any different for adult learners? Every story is designed to be both appealing and level-appropriate.

Use it or lose it

Don't get buried in your textbook. With our graded readers, you'll be able to recognize what you've already learned, and then synthesize that knowledge with new information simply by reading fun stories.
